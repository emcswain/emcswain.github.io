Introduction

Chiaro (company, organization, etc.)

Chiaro is an online jewlery retailer with a storefront location in San Francisco, CA. Chiaro specializes in quality, handmade pieces at an affordable price.With personalized engravings, their customers can choose the style, metal, and size of their jewelery, creating their own story.

Target audience

The target audience of Chiaro is primarily women, with an emphasis on young women, namely millennials and Gen Z. They would be the primary users of the site, but significant others, family members, etc. would also use the site, since jewelery is a common gift to give.

Chiaro's primary goal is to provide sustainable and luxury goods, without the markup of big-name jewlery brands. Chiaro wants customers to feel their pieces are special and worth wearing everyday, not just for special occasions. The brand also wants to emphasize its sustainability practices, with it using recycled metals and practicing made to order production.
